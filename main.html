Why I want to become a developer?

Creativity: Contrary to some opinions, coding is often a very creative process. Whether you’re designing a website, developing an app, or building a game,there's a lot of room for creativity. High Demand & Job Opportunities: Developers are in high demand in almost every industry,from tech companies to healthcare, finance, entertainment, and beyond. This means you have a wide range of job opportunities available to you. Constant Learning: Technology is always evolving, so being a developer means you’re always learning and growing. If you like to stay challenged and keep your skills sharp, this can be a major draw. Flexibility: Many development jobs offer flexible working conditions, such as remote work, freelancing, or even starting your own business. Impact: As a developer, you can have a direct impact on the way people live and work by creating tools, apps, websites, or systems that millions of people might use.
